Overview


At fitness first, you get exclusive access to knowledgeable teams, new technology and world class clubs. We have seen many changes and have come a long way since our humble beginnings to become one of the largest fitness brands in the region, but our purpose remains clear: we are the fitness leaders who inspire people to go further in life.

Here at our Westgate branch, we are easily accessbible at the Jurong East transport hub. Members can look forward to special features at the club, such as a stunning swimming pool with a landscaped deck and a sauna to relax at after a great workout.
